THANKS FOR YOUR INTEREST.
I'm a hobbyist photographer who began shooting 35mm film and later picked up digital. I try to compose and post process each and every photo to make sure I achieve the result I intended right out of the camera. I humbly shot 35mm, hence my approach to photography is naturalistic; playing with natural light and shadows, and engaging with color and tones to achieve the same aesthetic and feel that film offers. This is my way of bringing the nostalgia and emotions of the memories frozen into a single frame. If that is what you're looking for, then I may be able to help you.
