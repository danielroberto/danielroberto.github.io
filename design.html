Design

I'm currently an undergrad at University of California at San Diego, majoring in Cognitive Science, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction. I have taken classes in cognitive design, cognitive ethnography, interaction design, and design engineering while at UCSD. I currently a design intern for a startup company called BrewNGo. I also like to design and develop small projects myself and with friends.
HTML/CSS

This website you're on is my personal portfolio meant to showcase my skills in design as well as photography. This is probably the most coding-heavy project I've done. I designed this with a mobile-first approach, so that whatever device you're using, it should display responsively and seamlessly. This site is a reflection of my self-taught understanding of HTML and CSS.
